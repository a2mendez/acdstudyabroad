Itineraries

Eligibility

  • Minimum 2.5 GPA on a 4.0 scale
  • 18 years of age
  • At least sophomore standing (second year) at time of participation
  • Good standing at home and school

Schedule and credits

Dates and activities are subject to change without prior notice.

Spring Semester 2017

Credits: 15 semester / 22.5 quarter hours

Dates Activity
January 12: Departure from the U.S.
January 13: Arrival in Dublin, Ireland (airport code DUB) / Airport transfer and move-in
January 14-15: Orientation / Welcome Dinner / City Tour
January 16: Spring semester (1/16/17 – 5/5/17) classes begin
January 20: Last day to change course schedule (add/drop) for Spring semester
January 28: Day excursion to Glendalough
February 11-12: Overnight excursion to Galway and the Cliffs of Moher
March 4: Day excursion to Bray Head
March 13-17: Spring Break “Reading Week” – No classes
April 1-2: Overnight excursion to Belfast and Giant's Causeway (Northern Ireland)
April 13: Last day of classes for Spring semester / Farewell Dinner
April 14: Good Friday – College closed
April 17: Good Friday – College closed
April 18-21: Revision Week
April 24: Examination period (4/24/17 – 5/4/17) begins
January 12: Departure from the U.S.
May 1: May Bank Holiday – College closed
May 4: Examination period ends
May 5: Program ends / Move-out / Return to U.S. or travel independently
