About Us

Founded in 1993, American College Dublin (ACD) is a private, not-for-profit, U.S. accredited institution of higher learning that is a constituent college of the Irish American University (IAMU). For more information, visit amcd.ie.

ACD partners with Lynn University in the U.S., which provides institutional relations, recruitment and payment processing for ACD Study abroad programs.

Lynn is an independent college based in Boca Raton, Florida, with approximately 3,000 students from 100 countries. U.S. News & World Report ranks Lynn among the top five most international schools in the country. For more information, visit lynn.edu.

Accreditation

ACD is accredited in both Ireland and the United States. U.S. accreditation is through the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. ACD is also a member of the Association of American International Colleges and Universities.

ACD Study Abroad programs have no academic affiliation with Lynn University.

The ACD Study Abroad advantage

A direct connection with the university
With the ACD Study Abroad program, you have a direct connection with university support systems in both Ireland and the U.S. We help you, guide you and advise you directly, every step of the way. Plus, our intimate size means plenty of personalized attention.

100% Dublin dedicated
Our location in the heart of historic Dublin has allowed us to plant deep roots in the city and to know its many nooks and crannies. Plus, our ever-helpful Irish staff members know all the things you need to know about day-to-day living in Dublin.
